COVID-19
Reopening Rules and Policies

WHAT DO OUR CUSTOMERS NEED TO KNOW:

Fancy Shmancy by Liana will be operating strictly following the guidelines provided by the Health Department and Salon Lofts in the state of Illinois and respectfully asking our customers to do the same.

EXTRA STEPS WE ARE TAKING AS A SMALL LOCAL BUSINESS:

1. For everyone's safety, each nail station is properly spaced out and has clear dividers with special openings for customers' hands.
2. All of our iPads will be used with safe-touch pens only, and disinfected in between each use.
3. A medical-grade sanitation service has been added after hours on a regular basis to raise the minimum requirements bar and provide the highest level of sanitation. The process involves using an EPA-registered, non-chemical sanitizer that's safe for kids and pets. It does not require any rinsing or wiping, powerful yet gentle for everyone around, proven to kill 99% of germs, viruses, bacteria, and fungus. The proof of this sanitation will be displayed in the salon.

YOUR APPOINTMENTS:

We are currently accepting appointments with the first-come-first-serve basis over a text message on 224-432-7454 an email. All of the appointments will be spaced out in a timely manner to prioritize everyone's health and safety. Please specify the exact service and any details or add-ons involving your appointment to respect everyone's booking slots. If you do have an existing appointment, you should receive a notification to reschedule via text message. Otherwise, please contact us.

CANCELLATION POLICY:

If you feel sick or experince any symptoms of COVID-19 please give us a call ahead and please stay home. Same day cancellations will be charged 50% of the service, no-show -100% of the service.

CAN WE ACCEPT WALK-INS?

We will not accept any walk-ins at the moment, until further notice. Please contact us with any questions or concerns prior to stopping by.

IS THE WAITING AREA AVAILABLE?

Your health and safety is our #1 priority. That's why we can't have anyone in our waiting area. If you do need to wait, please let the staff know you are here, and do so in your car. We will notify you via text and/call when to come in.

WE ARE RESPECTFULLY ASKING OUR CUSTOMERS TO FOLLOW ILLINOIS COVID-19 GUIDELINES, SUCH AS:

1. Wear a face-covering mask.
2. Wash hands for at least 20 seconds as soon as you enter the salon.
3. No physical contact with anyone or anything, including your phone.
4. If you don't feel well, have a fever of 100.4 or above, please let us know and stay home. Take your temperature prior to coming in.

Feel free to reach out with questions and /or concerns at any point by calling us at 224-432-7454.
